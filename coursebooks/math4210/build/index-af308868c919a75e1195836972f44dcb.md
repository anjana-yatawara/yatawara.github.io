# Practice games

Every chapter has a short practice game: ten rounds mixing new material from
that chapter with review rounds sampled from earlier chapters. Each round
ends with a one-paragraph explanation of the answer, and a wrong answer
recommends exactly where to reread. Games are self-contained pages: no
account, no external requests, and they work on a phone.
